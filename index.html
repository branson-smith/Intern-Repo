Sign Up for Service Days

Give back and make a difference. IHS Markit encourages colleagues to participate in volunteer activities by providing the benefit of paid time off to volunteer, also referred to as Volunteer Time Off (VTO). Colleagues may use VTO to contribute their time and talents to recognized charities, causes and not-for-profit organizations that make a positive difference in deepening community connections. Community is not defined as just local community, but rather encompasses the global community.
